Legal

Data Privacy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

1. Privacy Commitment

Cambobia recognizes the importance of responsible data handling across its website and product ecosystem. We aim to collect only the information reasonably needed for communication, service delivery, security, and business operations.

2. Data Minimization

We seek to limit the collection and retention of personal information to what is relevant for product inquiries, support, account administration, operational services, and legal compliance.

3. Access and Control

Where appropriate and subject to operational or legal limits, individuals may request information about data associated with their direct relationship with Cambobia and may request corrections or updates.

4. Retention

Cambobia retains information only for as long as reasonably necessary for legitimate business purposes, service continuity, security, record-keeping, or compliance obligations.

5. Protection Measures

Reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards may be used to protect personal and business information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Product-Specific Data

Individual Cambobia products may have additional privacy or operational terms depending on their specific use case, such as messaging, learning, point of sale, or micro-lending workflows.

7. Contact

For data privacy requests or questions, contact contact@cambobia.com.
