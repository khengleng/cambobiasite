Legal

Data Privacy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

This page summarizes CamboBIA’s broader approach to responsible data handling, privacy expectations, and operational protection across the website and related product ecosystem. CamboBIA refers to Cambodia Boutique Investment Advisory.

1. Privacy Commitment

CamboBIA recognizes the importance of responsible data handling across its website and product ecosystem. We aim to collect only the information reasonably needed for communication, service delivery, security, and business operations.

2. Data Minimization

We seek to limit the collection and retention of personal information to what is relevant for product inquiries, support, account administration, operational services, and legal compliance.

3. Purpose Limitation

CamboBIA aims to use information for specific business, service, support, security, and compliance purposes rather than for unrelated or excessive secondary uses.

4. Access and Control

Where appropriate and subject to operational or legal limits, individuals may request information about data associated with their direct relationship with CamboBIA and may request corrections or updates.

5. Retention

CamboBIA retains information only for as long as reasonably necessary for legitimate business purposes, service continuity, security, record-keeping, or compliance obligations.

6. Protection Measures

Reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards may be used to protect personal and business information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

7. Third-Party Service Providers

CamboBIA may use third-party hosting, messaging, analytics, communications, or operational service providers where appropriate. These providers may process information on CamboBIA’s behalf under operational, contractual, or legal controls that are appropriate to the relationship.

8. Product-Specific Data

Individual CamboBIA products may have additional privacy or operational terms depending on their specific use case, such as messaging, learning, point of sale, or micro-lending workflows.

9. International Handling

Information may be stored or processed in environments that operate across different countries depending on the hosting, platform, and service-provider structure used to support CamboBIA’s operations.

10. Security and Incident Response

CamboBIA seeks to maintain reasonable safeguards and operational controls to identify, reduce, and respond to security risks affecting information handled through the website or its related platform environment.

11. Contact

For data privacy requests or questions, contact contact@cambobia.com.
