Messenger Hub msghub.cambobia.com

MSGHub

A business messenger hub for sending campaigns, announcements, and customer communication across WhatsApp, Telegram, and Signal from one platform.

  • Send promotions and business updates from one place
  • Reach customers across multiple chat channels
  • Reduce repeated manual messaging work
Learning Platform Curriculum + Quiz Support

AI Learning Center

A learning and quiz platform for banking professionals and students using international, Cambridge, and Cambodian Ministry of Education curricula.

  • Banking training and knowledge reinforcement
  • Exam practice from Grade 2 to Grade 12
  • Structured revision through guided quizzes
Micro-SME Platform cxdot.cambobia.com

CXDot

A platform for micro and small businesses to sell through Telegram, manage stock, and handle point-of-sale activity in one system.

  • Take orders through Telegram bots
  • Manage inventory and day-to-day sales
  • Support point-of-sale workflows for small teams
Micro-Lending Platform dbank.cambobia.com

DBank

A micro-lending platform that helps lenders manage borrowers, track loans, and keep lending records organized.

  • Keep borrower and loan records in one place
  • Track lending activity with better structure
  • Support daily micro-finance operations
Focus Game pairmeup.cambobia.com

Pair Me Up

A web-based memory and focus game that helps learners improve concentration, attention, and recall through short matching activities.

  • Encourages focus and memory practice
  • Simple to access from any browser
  • Works well alongside learning experiences
